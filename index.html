Primary Research

Initial inspection of Operation Tiny Home's original website design and further research into what the organization stands for

Operation Tiny Home is a nonprofit that aims at helping those in unstable housing situations and strive to provide high-quailty tiny housing solutions while empowering participants with work training simultaneously. Providing aid to veterans as well as other civil service workers who are either homeless or in an unstable living situation that meets the organization's need requirements.

Audience and Goals

Research describing the Determined Audiences, Operation Tiny Home's Overarching and Specific Goals. Including interviews with members of the two main audience categories.

After completing extensive research about Operation Tiny Home and their programs, I was able to distinguish who their audiences are. This research is essential in order to create an effective web design based on the understanding of thier catered audiences.

Solutions

The design choices made to improve Operation Tiny Home's current website layout.

The original layout was hard to navigate to get to the the viewer's intended information. To resolve this, I created a simple color hierarchy to direct audiences to the information that pertains to them. Overall simplicity became a goal for the redesign.
