Design Challenge

Framework of the design problem that needs to be solved reguarding the original design of Operation Tiny Home's website.

What is the design problem that I, as the designer, am trying to solve?

Improving the lifestyle of those in an unstable environment.

The Design Question

How can the website inspire people from different backgrounds to get involved with this nonprofit.

The Ultmiate Impact

To provide stable living environments through tiny homes and teach career skills to help those in need be in a stable environment.

Possible solutions

  • Visual elements that lead user throughout the website
  • Separate tabs for different audiences and their respective information
  • Clearer navigation bar setup
  • More use of color throughout site
  • Welcoming and trustworthy typeface choices

Context and Constraints

Nationwide audience with different needs for the website. Each group comes from different social and economic standings but make each group feel like they are being addressed and can relate to.

The Design Question (restated)

How can the website bring people together and inspire them to get involved with Operation Tiny Home.
